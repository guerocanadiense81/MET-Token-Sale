How to See Your MET Tokens

After purchasing, you must add MET as a custom token in your MetaMask wallet to see your balance.

  1. Open MetaMask and ensure you are on the Binance Smart Chain network.
  2. Click the "Import tokens" link at the bottom of the "Assets" tab.
  3. Select the "Custom Token" tab.
  4. Paste the MET Token Contract Address:
    0xb80b92Be7402E1e2D3189fff261D672D8104b322
  5. The "Token Symbol" (MET) and "Decimals" (18) should auto-fill.
  6. Click "Add Custom Token," then "Import Tokens."
